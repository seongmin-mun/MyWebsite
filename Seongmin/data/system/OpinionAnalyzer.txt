<p>The rapid development of the Web 2.0 era has generated a huge amount of online word-of-mouth information, which has influenced the society in various fields. Effects of Word of Mouth on movies, in particular, has become the new standards to evaluate a movie that affects potential audiencesâ€™ decision. Therefore, this research proposes a methodology to analyze the responses of movie audiences with the visualization.</p>
