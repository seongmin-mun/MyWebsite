<p>Predictive analysis is based on a probabilistic learning algorithm called pattern recognition or machine learning. Therefore, if users want to extract more information from the data, they are required high statistical knowledge. This study conducted statistical data analyses and visual data analyses to supplement the prediction analysis's weakness.</p>
